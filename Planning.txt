Pairs Game


Difficulty level
- select the amount of cards that are in play
- limit the amount of moves the player is allowed to make before they complete the Game
- time limit to complete game
- card reveal timing (shorter time is harder)


On screen

display level - 1 to 10
display move counter - the less moves the higher the score


Game grid
box of squares that when clicked reveal a symbol for a short amount of time then flip back over
when two matching cards are flipped one after another, this is a pair... those cards are greyed out and stay flipped over.

(game within a game, each mini game has two moves only... this will stop move 2 from previous game and move 1 from new game being a match)




logic
level 1
10 squares (5 pairs of symbols)
randomly place the individual symbols across the 10 squares
